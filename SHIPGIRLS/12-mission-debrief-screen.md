# Shipgirls 12 - Mission Debrief Screen

- Screen type: `post-mission results`
- Location framing: `quiet command room after operation`
- Functional purpose: Review objectives, breaches, fleet losses, recovered data, and standing within the confederation.
- Visible content summary: The screen combines a mission ledger with tactical snapshots, system logs, and repaired communication transcripts. Lamps, consoles, and distant harbor or orbital traffic imply the fleet is between operations rather than inside active combat.
- Interface details: Summary panels break down route efficiency, successful terminal intrusions, robotic assets preserved, and doctrine changes unlocked. The player can continue to the next operation, inspect logs, or move directly into refit planning.
- Vehicle or device presence: Debrief charts compare performance across carrier, aircraft, and spacecraft layers of the mission.
