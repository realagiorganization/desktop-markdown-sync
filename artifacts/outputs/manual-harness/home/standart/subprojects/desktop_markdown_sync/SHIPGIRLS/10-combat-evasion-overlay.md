# Shipgirls 10 - Combat Evasion Overlay

- Screen type: `high-alert survival mode`
- Location framing: `missile and interceptor engagement zone`
- Functional purpose: Evade incoming attacks while preserving command links and keeping automated craft under control.
- Visible content summary: Alarm glyphs flash over the battlefield as the screen fills with tracers, contrails, or orbital weapon arcs depending on vehicle class. The camera shakes harder than in standard navigation, emphasizing damage risk and deteriorating control.
- Interface details: Evasion controls become larger and more aggressive, with flares, shield timing, decoy deployment, and route-break prompts on screen. A live terminal strip shows whether the player’s command net is degrading under enemy jamming.
- Vehicle or device presence: The overlay adapts to carrier, aircraft, or spacecraft telemetry but always keeps direct vehicle handling visible.
