# Bhakti 04 - Shrine Interaction Screen

- Screen type: `context interaction`
- Platform framing: `dual-screen devotional scene`
- Functional purpose: Approach a shrine, inspect offerings, and begin text or ritual activities.
- Visible content summary: The top screen presents a close shrine view with lamps, flowers, fabric hangings, and a central murti. The bottom screen shows contextual touch icons for inspect, offer, listen, and study.
- Interface details: Touch prompts change based on shrine state, time of day, and progress. A small ribbon can indicate whether new verses or translations are available from this location.
- Device presence: Stylus cues on the lower screen direct each interaction.
