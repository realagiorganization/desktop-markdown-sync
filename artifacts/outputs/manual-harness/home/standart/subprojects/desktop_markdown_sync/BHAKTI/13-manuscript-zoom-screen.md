# Bhakti 13 - Manuscript Zoom Screen

- Screen type: `document inspection`
- Platform framing: `top screen close reading, bottom screen zoom tools`
- Functional purpose: Examine manuscript pages, marginalia, and highlighted passages in detail.
- Visible content summary: The top screen shows a textured manuscript page with careful line spacing, decorative borders, and faded notation marks. The bottom screen holds zoom, pan, and overlay toggles.
- Interface details: Players can switch between raw manuscript view, clean transcription, and translated overlay. Tapping a seal or margin note can open related commentary or historical context.
- Device presence: Zoom and pan are controlled through touch buttons and drag gestures on the lower screen.
