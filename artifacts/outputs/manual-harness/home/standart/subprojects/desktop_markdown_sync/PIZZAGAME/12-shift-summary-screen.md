# PizzaGame 12 - Shift Summary Screen

- Screen type: `end-of-day results`
- Location framing: `small rental room above a shop`
- Functional purpose: Review earnings, heat, customer ratings, stolen data, and district progress after a shift.
- Visible content summary: A desk fan, charging cables, pizza receipts, and a helmet sit around the player’s phone and compact laptop. The results panel breaks the shift into deliveries completed, hacks attempted, systems breached, and near-misses during scooter travel.
- Interface details: Charts show income versus repair cost, while a message feed summarizes which terminals were cracked and which contacts unlocked new jobs. The player can continue to the next day, inspect detailed logs, or spend points on scooter and device upgrades.
- Vehicle or device presence: The scooter is partially visible through the open window below, and the summary is framed through phone and terminal UI elements to keep the courier-hacker identity consistent.
