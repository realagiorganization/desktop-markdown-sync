# Bhakti 11 - Audio Recitation Player

- Screen type: `audio playback and practice`
- Platform framing: `top screen art, bottom screen controls`
- Functional purpose: Listen to recitations, loop segments, and practice memorization.
- Visible content summary: The top screen shows rotating artwork of temple scenes, manuscripts, or murtis related to the current recitation. The bottom screen presents a player with progress bar, loop markers, and phrase jump buttons.
- Interface details: Users can set in and out points, bookmark difficult passages, and switch between narrator voices. A small indicator shows whether transcript and translation overlays are available for the selected audio.
- Device presence: Playback is handled with stylus-friendly controls on the bottom screen.
