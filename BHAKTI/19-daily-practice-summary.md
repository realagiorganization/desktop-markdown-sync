# Bhakti 19 - Daily Practice Summary

- Screen type: `session recap`
- Platform framing: `top screen visual recap, bottom screen metrics`
- Functional purpose: Summarize progress made during a study day and suggest next steps.
- Visible content summary: The top screen shows a calm end-of-day temple view with the currently favored murti or manuscript theme. The bottom screen reports verses reviewed, annotations added, chants practiced, and puzzles cleared.
- Interface details: Recommended next activities appear as touch buttons for review weak areas, continue a route, or replay a recitation. Small badges indicate streaks and newly unlocked glossary entries.
- Device presence: The summary actions are presented as large DS-style buttons on the bottom screen.
