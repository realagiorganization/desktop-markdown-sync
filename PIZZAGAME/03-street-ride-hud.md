# PizzaGame 03 - Street Ride HUD

- Screen type: `moment-to-moment driving gameplay`
- Location framing: `busy Pattaya side street`
- Functional purpose: Ride to a destination while balancing speed, traffic avoidance, and mission timers.
- Visible content summary: The camera sits just behind the scooter as the player weaves past tuk-tuks, parked motorbikes, and food carts. Neon signs and wet pavement reflections make the route readable even at night.
- Interface details: Vehicle controls are always visible with throttle, brake, lean, and handbrake prompts on screen. A compact mini-map shows the next turn, a timer tracks pizza freshness, and an incoming call banner can trigger a mobile hack objective without fully pausing the ride.
- Vehicle or device presence: The scooter dashboard shows speed, fuel, and engine strain. A phone mount above the handlebars mirrors GPS guidance and short terminal alerts.
